Product Story

Each bowl is carefully shaped by skilled artisans using traditional woodworking techniques. The oak is sourced from sustainably managed forests, then hand-finished with natural oils to bring out the wood's beautiful grain and ensure food safety.

Care Instructions

Hand wash with mild soap and warm water. Dry immediately. Apply food-safe mineral oil monthly to maintain the wood's luster. Avoid soaking or placing in dishwasher.

Our Story

Pine Lab was born from a simple belief: that the objects we live with should honor both nature and craft. We partner with skilled artisans and sustainable material sources to create pieces that bring warmth, beauty, and intention into your home.

Every item in our collection is thoughtfully designed and made by hand, ensuring quality that lasts for generations. We believe in slow production, fair wages, and materials that give back to the earth.
